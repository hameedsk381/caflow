# 📘 CAFlow User Guide: Premium Compliance & Practice Management

Welcome to the new and improved **CAFlow**. This guide covers the advanced features implemented in version 2.1.0, designed to make your firm's compliance workflow 100% automated and audit-ready.

---

## 📅 1. Statutory Due-Date Engine
CAFlow now automatically calculates all critical Indian statutory deadlines (GST, ITR, ROC, TDS, etc.) for your clients.

- **Automated Population**: As soon as you add a client with their registration type, the system populates their upcoming deadlines.
- **Visual Status**:
  - 🔴 **Red**: Due in ≤ 3 days (Urgent).
  - 🟡 **Yellow**: Due in 4–7 days (Upcoming).
  - 🟢 **Green**: Filed.
- **Dashboard Widget**: The "Upcoming Deadlines" widget on your main dashboard provides a prioritized view of the most urgent tasks across your entire firm.

---

## 🔔 2. Automated Reminders & Notifications
Never miss a deadline again with our proactive notification system.

- **Proactive Alerts**: CAFlow scans for deadlines 7 days in advance and queues reminders.
- **Communication Channels**:
  - **Email**: Detailed notifications sent to staff and optionally to clients.
  - **WhatsApp**: Concise, mobile-first reminders for instant action.
- **Preferences**: Go to **Settings → Notifications** to toggle channels and adjust your reminder window (default is 7 days).

---

## 🌉 3. Portal Sync Bridge
The **Portal Sync Bridge** is a first-of-its-kind automated bridge between CAFlow and government portals (MCA/GST).

- **Vault Security**: All portal credentials are stored in an encrypted **Password Vault**. Only authorized admins can trigger a sync.
- **One-Click Verification**: In the Client Details page, click **"Sync Portal"** to verify return filing status in real-time.
- **Auto-Mapping**: When a sync confirms a filing (e.g., GSTR-3B filed on GST portal), CAFlow **automatically** updates your internal compliance records to "Filed," saving you manual data entry time.
- **Sync History**: A full audit log of every sync attempt (success or failure) is available for transparency.

---

## 📜 4. Audit Logs (Firm-Wide Activity)
For compliance-heavy firms, transparency is key.

- **Activity Trail**: Every critical action (Created Client, Updated Filing, Revealed Password, Sent Reminder) is logged with a timestamp and the actor's name.
- **Audit Dashboard**: Navigate to **Audit Logs** in the sidebar to search and filter through your firm's entire operational history.
- **Compliance Ready**: Export these logs for internal audits or to demonstrate process integrity to clients.

---

## 📈 5. Real-Time Insights & Financials
The dashboard now provides a "Practice Pulse" based on live data.

- **Practice Overview**: Real-time filing rates, open notices, and team utilization.
- **Financial Pulse**: Revenue tracking, collection rates, and work-in-progress (WIP) valuation.
- **Historical Trends**: View your firm's growth and compliance performance over time with interactive charts.
